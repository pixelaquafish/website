What is OpenSea?

OpenSea is a marketplace for NFTs.
It was founded on December 20, 2017 by Devin Finzer and Alex Atallah in New York City.
Users can generate NFTs for free on OpenSea and offer them for direct purchase or auction.
OpenSea is based on the Ethereum ERC-721 standard. A crypto wallet such as B. Bitski or MetaMask required.
